Our Story

Warm & Crumbly was born from a simple belief: that the best moments in life are shared over something warm, sweet, and made with love. What started as a tiny kitchen experiment in a Brooklyn apartment has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery, where every cookie tells a story of comfort, craftsmanship, and community.

We believe in the magic of real ingredients — European-style butter, rich dark chocolate, and a pinch of nostalgia in every batch. Our bakery is a place where the scent of freshly baked cookies drifts onto the sidewalk, inviting neighbors and visitors alike to pause, indulge, and feel at home.

Rooted in Brooklyn, Shared with Love

From our small storefront in the heart of Brooklyn, we handcraft each cookie and curated cookie box with the same care we'd offer a friend. Every order is an invitation to experience the warmth of our kitchen, no matter where you are.

Baked with Heart, Rooted in Brooklyn

The Warmth of Home

Every batch we pull from the oven is rooted in the comfort of a home kitchen. We believe a cookie isn't just a treat—it's a moment of pause, a memory of grandma's table, and the simplest way to share a smile across a crowded counter.

Handcrafted, Never Rushed

We mix our doughs in small batches, fold in the chocolate by hand, and watch each tray with a baker's patience. No shortcuts, no assembly lines—just the slow, deliberate craft that transforms butter, flour, and sugar into something worth waiting for.

Thoughtful Gifting, Brooklyn-Style

Our curated cookie boxes are designed to feel like a gift from a neighbor. Wrapped with care and filled with seasonal flavors, each box carries the spirit of our block in Brooklyn—generous, personal, and always baked fresh for the people you love.

Baked in Small Batches, Wrapped with Care

Every box that leaves our Brooklyn kitchen starts with a rhythm that can’t be rushed. We mix dough in small, deliberate batches — never more than a dozen cookies at a time — so each one gets the attention it deserves. As the scent of browned butter and melted chocolate fills the room, we pull trays from the oven at the exact moment the edges turn golden and the centers stay soft. The result is a cookie that feels like it was baked just for you, because it was.

Once cooled, each cookie is nestled into our signature gift boxes by hand. We layer parchment between the layers, tie the box with a linen ribbon, and tuck a handwritten tasting note inside — because the unboxing should feel as thoughtful as the baking itself. From our oven to your doorstep, every step is designed to deliver warmth, crumble, and a little bit of Brooklyn magic.
